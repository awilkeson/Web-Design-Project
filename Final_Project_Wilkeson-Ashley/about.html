About Ashley

A Bit of Background

I'm from Northwest Indiana and love spending my summers at Lake Michigan. I'm finishing up my junior year at Indiana University majoring in Computer Informatics and minors in Cyber-Security and Human-Centered Computing. Last summer I interned with the University of Notre Dame's OIT Department in Notre Dame, Indiana. I enjoy spending my down time listening to music and playing with my cat, Jasmine(Jazz).

Looking to the Future

One of my main goals in life is to inspire others, especially other women and young girls. I hope that whatever job I'm in or company I'm a part of I am able to inspire those around me. I recently saw a quote that mentioned how people that travel back in time are always worried that a small change could impact the present, but those in the present don't think that a small change could change the future; I hope to make those small changes myself and inspire others to do so as well.

Outside Interests

Music has always been a central part of my life, and I'm currently in the IU Marching Hundred playing the piccolo. I've had many opportunities to travel in college, and I hope that I can continue that after graduation. Although it's always nerve wracking to step outside of my comfort zone, I enjoy it because it allows me to expand my experiences.
