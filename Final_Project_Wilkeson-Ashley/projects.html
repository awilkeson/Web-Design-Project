Cougar Chronicle

I was on the highscool newspaper staff a few years ago and wanted to start a website for them, I designed one then but it was never published. Last summer I designed one for them to use with Muse and it was in need of updates after a recent crash. So for I360, a web design class at IUB, I redesigned a website for them to use.

Although they will not be using this design, due to their need to use Muse for ease of use for students who do not know HTML/CSS, I continue to work with the New Prairie High School Newspaper advisor and staff to create a website that has the modern, historic feel they want on a platform that they can update.
