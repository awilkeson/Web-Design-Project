Serious Tech for Serious Hikers

Project Description

Interactive Design, Inc. is a company searching for new solutions to reach their audience in new ways. They provide software and services to a wide variety of clients in many industries. The title of this project is Serious Tech for Serious Hikers and the focus is how to “enable hikers in a national park to navigate, stay safe, learn of impending weather and other issues, to seek emergency help and so on,” as given by Interactive Design Inc.’s Chief Innovative Officer Sally Lee(provided by Professor). The target customers for this tasked project would be the National Park Service that wishes to develop content and services for various industries using mobile technologies, but our end users would be hikers.

Happy Hikers Product

For this project our team, Happy Hikers, settled on a concept of using glasses to create an augmented reality for hikers in which they receive a slew of functions: directions, severe weather prompts, large movement in surroundings alerts, as well as a few sound-oriented functions, such as sound-enhancing features to amplify nature’s serene sounds.
